OperatorStack

Data Deletion Instructions

If you want OperatorStack to delete personal data associated with you, send a request to treyw@obsynix.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request.

Include in Your Request

  • Your full name
  • The email address you used with OperatorStack
  • A short description of what you want deleted

What Happens Next

  • We may verify your identity before processing the request.
  • We will review the request and delete eligible personal data that we are not required to keep for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or transaction-record purposes.
  • We will confirm once the request has been processed.

Questions

For help with deletion requests or privacy questions, contact treyw@obsynix.com.
